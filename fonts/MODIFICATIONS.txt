Font modifications
==================

AeonGothic-Regular.ttf
  Derived from League Gothic (Copyright 2010 Caroline Hadilaksono & Micah Rich,
  The League of Moveable Type), SIL Open Font License 1.1 (see licenses/LeagueGothic.txt).
  Change: asciitilde (U+007E) recentered ~355 units down onto the math axis
  (it sat above cap height). Renamed from "League Gothic" to "AeonGothic" as required
  by the OFL Reserved Font Name clause. Remains under OFL 1.1.

AeonCondensed-Regular.ttf
  Base: Roboto Condensed (Copyright 2011 Google Inc.), Apache License 2.0.
  Changes:
   - Added vulgar fractions U+2153 U+2154 U+2155 U+2156 U+2157 U+2158
     (1/3 2/3 1/5 2/5 3/5 4/5), composed from Roboto's own numerator/denominator
     figures + fraction bar (matching the pre-existing 1/4 1/2 3/4 1/8 3/8 5/8 7/8).
   - Merged ~1340 glyphs that Roboto Condensed lacked (extended Latin/Greek/Cyrillic/IPA,
     punctuation, super/subscripts, currency, letterlike, Roman numerals, arrows, math,
     geometric shapes, symbols), sourced from Noto Sans and Fira Sans (OFL-1.1) and one
     glyph from Open Sans (Apache-2.0), decomposed to outlines at 2048 UPM.
   - Original glyphs: U+2605 (black star) and U+25B3 (white up triangle) drawn from
     scratch; U+2161 (Roman numeral two) composed from two capital I; U+2661 U+2236
     U+274D U+2727 U+2612 U+29B5 mapped to their nearest existing glyph.
  Renamed from "Roboto Condensed" to "AeonCondensed" (avoids the source names/trademarks).
  Because it now contains OFL-1.1 glyphs, the combined font is licensed under the
  SIL Open Font License 1.1 (see licenses/SIL Open Font License.txt); all source
  copyright notices are retained in the font's name table.
